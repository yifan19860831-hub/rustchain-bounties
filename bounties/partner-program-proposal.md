## Executive Summary

This proposal establishes a comprehensive **RustChain Partner Program** to accelerate ecosystem growth through strategic partnerships. The program creates clear tiers, incentives, and pathways for organizations and individuals to contribute to RustChain's success while earning RTC rewards.

---

## Partner Tiers

### 🥉 **Bronze Partner** (Entry Level)
**Requirements:**
- Public mention of RustChain in documentation or website
- Link to rustchain.org from project resources
- Active participation in RustChain Discord/community
- Minimum 1 integration or content contribution

**Benefits:**
- Bronze Partner badge for website/display
- Listing on RustChain Partners page
- Early access to feature announcements
- **Reward:** 50-100 RTC one-time + 5 RTC/month stipend

---

### 🥈 **Silver Partner** (Active Contributor)
**Requirements:**
- All Bronze requirements, plus:
- Deep integration with RustChain (API, SDK, or tool)
- Monthly content contribution (blog, tutorial, video)
- Active bounties participation (minimum 2/month)
- Referral of 3+ new contributors

**Benefits:**
- Silver Partner badge + co-marketing opportunities
- Featured spot on RustChain homepage
- Direct line to core team for support
- Speaking opportunities at RustChain events
- **Reward:** 200-500 RTC one-time + 20 RTC/month stipend + 5% referral bonus

---

### 🥇 **Gold Partner** (Ecosystem Leader)
**Requirements:**
- All Silver requirements, plus:
- Strategic integration (major platform or tool)
- Dedicated RustChain advocate on team
- Quarterly joint initiatives (webinars, events, campaigns)
- Consistent bounty contributions (minimum 5/month)
- Referral of 10+ active contributors

**Benefits:**
- Gold Partner badge + premium co-marketing
- Joint press releases and case studies
- Advisory board seat for RustChain roadmap
- Revenue sharing on referred bounties (10%)
- **Reward:** 1000-2500 RTC one-time + 100 RTC/month stipend + 10% referral bonus + governance voting rights

---

### 💎 **Diamond Partner** (Strategic Alliance)
**Requirements:**
- All Gold requirements, plus:
- Enterprise-level integration or partnership
- Multi-year commitment to RustChain ecosystem
- Significant resource allocation (team, budget, marketing)
- Thought leadership in RustChain community

**Benefits:**
- Diamond Partner badge + exclusive branding
- Strategic alliance announcement
- Board observer rights
- Custom partnership terms
- **Reward:** 5000-10000 RTC one-time + 500 RTC/month stipend + 15% referral bonus + governance voting rights + revenue sharing opportunities

## Application Process

### Step 1: Express Interest
- Comment on partnership issue or open new issue
- Brief description of partnership type and goals
- Initial contribution or proof of capability

### Step 2: Application Review
- Core team reviews within 7 days
- Discussion of mutual expectations
- Tier assignment based on commitment level

### Step 3: Onboarding
- Sign partnership agreement (simple markdown commitment)
- Receive partner badge and assets
- Listed on partners page
- Access to partner-only channels

### Step 4: Ongoing Engagement
- Monthly check-ins and progress reviews
- Quarterly tier assessments (can upgrade/downgrade)
- Continuous bounty participation
- Joint marketing activities

## Success Metrics

Partners are evaluated quarterly on:

1. **Contribution Quality** (40%)
   - Bounties completed
   - Integrations shipped
   - Content published

2. **Ecosystem Impact** (30%)
   - New users referred
   - GitHub stars driven
   - Social engagement

3. **Community Engagement** (20%)
   - Discord activity
   - Event participation
   - Help provided to newcomers

4. **Consistency** (10%)
   - Monthly activity
   - Long-term commitment
   - Reliability

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Partner Inactivity
- **Mitigation:** Quarterly reviews, tier downgrades for inactive partners
- **Action:** Automatic downgrade after 60 days of inactivity

### Risk 2: Budget Depletion
- **Mitigation:** Cap on partner count per tier, performance-based renewals
- **Action:** Monthly budget review, adjust stipends if needed

### Risk 3: Quality Control
- **Mitigation:** Application review process, probationary period
- **Action:** 30-day probation for new partners, core team approval required

### Risk 4: Misaligned Incentives
- **Mitigation:** Clear success metrics, regular communication
- **Action:** Quarterly partner surveys, feedback loops with core team
