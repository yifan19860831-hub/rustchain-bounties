## 📝 引用示例

### 在论文中引用 RustChain

**示例段落:**
> "本研究使用了 RustChain 区块链网络进行实验 (Scott & Elyan Labs, 2026)。RustChain 采用 Proof-of-Antiquity 共识机制，通过硬件指纹验证来奖励复古计算设备。"

**英文示例:**
> "This study utilized the RustChain blockchain network for experiments (Scott & Elyan Labs, 2026). RustChain employs a Proof-of-Antiquity consensus mechanism that rewards vintage computing hardware through hardware fingerprint verification."
