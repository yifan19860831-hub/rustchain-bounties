## Historical Context
- **ORDVAC** (Ordnance Discrete Variable Automatic Computer)
- Built: 1952, University of Illinois for Ballistic Research Laboratory
- Technology: 2,178 vacuum tubes
- Memory: Williams tubes (1024 × 40 bits)
- Architecture: IAS (von Neumann)
- Unique feature: KSNJFL hex notation instead of ABCDEF
